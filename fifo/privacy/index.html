Privacy Policy

FIFO: Pantry & Expiry Tracker

Provider: CNTRL Design (“we”, “us”)
Last updated: 26 June 2026
Contact: support@cntrldesign.com

FIFO helps you track the food you already have, reduce waste, and plan groceries. We built FIFO to need as little of your data as possible. This policy explains what the app handles and where it goes.

The short version: Your pantry, grocery list, recipes, and history live only on your device. We have no account system and never ask for your name or email. The only things that leave your device are (1) photos you choose to scan with AI, (2) a scanned barcode for product lookup, and (3) your subscription status — described below.
01

Information stored only on your device

Everything you enter or generate in normal use is saved locally on your device and is not transmitted to us:

  • Pantry items, quantities, locations, and expiry dates
  • Grocery / shopping lists
  • Saved and AI-generated recipes, and your taste preferences
  • Used / thrown-away history and money-saved figures
  • App settings (theme, text size)

If you delete the app, or use Reset app in Settings, this information is erased from your device. We keep no copy.

02

Information that leaves your device

a) AI photo scanning

When you choose to scan a receipt or a photo of your food, the image is resized and sent — over an encrypted connection — to our processing service (Supabase Edge Functions) and our AI provider (Anthropic, the Claude API) to identify the items in it. The same applies to text-only AI features (recipe ideas, grocery suggestions, waste tips), which send the relevant item names.

We do not store these images or the AI results on our servers; they are processed to generate your result and then discarded. Anthropic processes the request under its own terms and does not use it to train its models.

b) Barcode lookup

If you scan a product barcode, the barcode number is sent to Open Food Facts (an open product database) to fetch the product name. No personal information is included.

c) Fair-use rate limiting

So that AI features stay affordable and available to everyone, the app creates a random, anonymous identifier (via Supabase anonymous sign-in). It is not linked to your name, email, or Apple ID. We store only a daily count of AI requests against that identifier to enforce per-user and overall limits. Reinstalling the app generates a new identifier.

d) Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage and verify subscription status. RevenueCat assigns a random, anonymous purchase identifier and receives your purchase/subscription details and basic device information to keep your Premium access in sync. We never see your payment card details — Apple handles payment.

e) Diagnostics

Our servers keep limited technical error logs (for example, “the AI request failed”) to keep the service running. These do not contain your pantry contents or photos.

03

What we do NOT collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, or advertising identifiers. FIFO contains no advertising, no third-party analytics/tracking SDKs, and we never sell your data.

04

Third-party services

The services that may process data on our behalf, each under its own privacy policy:

  • Apple — in-app purchases / subscriptions
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Supabase — backend, anonymous authentication, rate limiting
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI image and text processing
  • Open Food Facts — barcode product lookup
05

Data retention

  • On-device data: kept until you delete it (Reset app) or uninstall the app.
  • Scanned images / AI text: not retained by us after processing.
  • Anonymous rate-limit counts: automatically roll over daily.
06

Your choices and deletion

  • Delete your data: use Reset app in the app’s Settings, or uninstall the app, to remove all information stored on your device.
  • Reset your anonymous identifier: reinstalling the app issues a new anonymous identifier.
  • Subscriptions: manage or cancel anytime in your Apple ID → Subscriptions settings.
  • For any privacy question or request, contact us at the email above.
07

Children’s privacy

FIFO is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

08

Security

Data sent off the device travels over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we limit what is sent and what is kept.

09

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will revise the “Last updated” date above and, for significant changes, note them in the app or its release notes.
